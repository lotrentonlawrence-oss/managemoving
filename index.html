Frequently Asked Questions

Do we have to book the full package, or can we choose just one phase?

Every service can be booked individually — a consultation only, sorting help only, or the full end-to-end package. We'll recommend what fits your timeline and budget.

What happens to items we don't want to keep?

We sort everything into Keep, Gift to Family, Sell, Donate, or Discard. Sellable items go to estate sale, online auction, or trusted antique dealers; the rest is donated or responsibly disposed of.

Where do donated items go?

If your family has a preferred place to donate, we will absolutely use that organization. Otherwise, we prioritize trusted local donation centers such as St. Vincent de Paul (SVDP), Asbury, and New Leash on Life to make sure donated items are distributed responsibly in our community.

How do you charge for liquidation?

Our 30% commission covers the full effort behind every sale: pricing research across auction and resale markets, cleaning and light repair to get each item in its best condition, professional photography and listing, and coordinating pickup or delivery — all aimed at getting your family top dollar.

Do you provide the movers, or do we need to hire our own?

We coordinate and supervise trusted, vetted third-party moving crews as part of White-Glove Move Management, so you have one point of contact for everything.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend 3–6 weeks' notice for a full project package, though we can accommodate tighter timelines when needed — just call us.
